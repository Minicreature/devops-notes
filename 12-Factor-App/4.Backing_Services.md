# 4. Backing Services

Here, in the example we integrated Redis as a Caching service to store the session related data. In the same way we will have a SMTP for handling email services. An S3 integration to store images.

Treat backing services as attached resources. This means whatever belongs to these services should not be located in app codebase. It should have its own codebase, may it runs on locally hosted or remotely hosted services. In case of this service goes down we just have to spin another instance & it should work as expected. EASY!!
